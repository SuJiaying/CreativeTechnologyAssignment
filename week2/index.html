Hello, Xiang Xiang!

This adorable photo comes from the Japanese fan-made blog Mainichi Panda. Click here to view the blog that contains this photo. On this website, you can also see photos of herparents and younger siblings.

Xiang Xiang is a female giant panda born on June 12, 2017 through natural mating in the Ueno Zoo in Japan. Her father is Ri Ri and mother is Shin Shin.

About pandas

The official name of the familiar black and white panda is the giant panda. Basically, they are fierce beasts like bears, but they are herbivorous and leisurely creatures that eat bamboo as their staple food.

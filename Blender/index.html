I want to design something like a lighting tool for a future shaped by Hebraic values and Nature as Sacred. Our current lighting tools are highly function-driven, so incorporating ecocentrism or non-human-centered functions could be interesting. The idea of nature worship reminds me of Art Deco and Art Nouveau aesthetics, which might serve as inspiration for my design.

The future society I envisioned is set around the mid-22nd century. By then, the Nordic region might develop a religion that worships natural elements, so I designed a shrine for honoring nature.

The shrine's silhouette is inspired by the snowdrop, a common flower in Northern Europe. The irregularly shaped light at the center symbolizes the natural elements people wish to worship.
